My dear children,

My first post...

Sunday 22nd March 2026

My dear children. I love you with all my heart. You fill my heart with joy. I do not know when and if you will read this, but I hope that you will read this one day. Life is fragile as it is precious. I will not be around forever, but I am hoping that my words will be. You are still young right now and there is so much I want to teach you. Thoughts go through my mind of what I want to say to you, but your little minds are not yet ready for some of the things I would like to instill in you. I am hoping that this blog may allow you to refer back to my words when you are older, all grown up and I am long gone from this world. I love you guys so much. I will write again soon.

I will always be with you in your hearts. With love always,forever and eternity, Mum.
